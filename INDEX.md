\# Prediction Market Operations Playbook



This repository documents the \*\*end-to-end operational lifecycle\*\* of an orderbook-based prediction market, covering market creation, live operations, risk management, oracle resolution, dispute handling, and post-market review.



It is designed as an \*\*ops reference\*\*, not theoretical research.
